# Photos, shipping, returns, scams, account health & cross-listing

## Photos & styling

**Display methods** (both research sources rate on-body display the top converter for vintage —
mannequin and live model trade the #1 spot depending on the piece; flat lay is the fast fallback)
- **Mannequin / ghost mannequin** — fitted/structured pieces; shows drape and fit; highest
  conversion for most vintage.
- **Live model / AI on-model** — best for high-value dresses & statement pieces; shows movement
  and era (especially strong on Depop/Poshmark).
- **Flat lay** — knits, tees, simple casuals; fast, but weaker for dresses/tailored.

**Photo checklist**
1. Prep: steam, lint-roll, button/zip, stuff sleeves & body for shape.
2. Light: natural daylight or daylight-balanced LED/softbox.
3. Shoot: front, back, side, brand/size tag, fabric close-up, flaw close-up, hem/cuff, scale
   (tape measure in frame), on-model/ghost mannequin.
4. Background: white/neutral for the main image; styled backgrounds OK for secondary (Depop/Poshmark).
5. Edit: remove background (Photoroom, Pixelcut), fix brightness/white-balance — **never** edit out flaws.
6. Upload up to **24 images + 1 video** — the current free limit (verified June 2026; eBay has
   been piloting an increase to 40, and variation listings still cap at 12 images *per variation*).
   Shoot each at **1600px+** on the long side; **no borders, text, or watermarks** on images.
7. Sequence: front → back → side → details → flaws → tags → scale → on-model.

**2025–2026:** AI on-model and ghost-mannequin images are now widely accepted. Honest flaw
documentation matters more than ever.

## Shipping & packaging

- **Calculated shipping** = default for new sellers (accurate).
- **Free shipping** can lift search ranking ~20–30% — build the cost into the price.
- Poly mailers for most apparel; bubble mailers for delicate/embellished; boxes for structured items.
- Weigh **after** packaging on a digital postal scale; measure dimensions for accurate cost.
  **USPS dimensional-weight rules are now strictly enforced** — enter real weight + dimensions or
  eBay will adjust the label and eat your margin.
- **eBay label discounts** run ~10–20% off retail USPS/UPS — always buy postage through eBay.

**Poly mailer sizing**
- 10×13" — tops, skirts, light dresses (up to ~15.9 oz)
- 12×15.5" — bulkier dresses, sweaters
- 14×17" — coats, multi-item orders

| Package | Weight | Service | Cost (2026) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Tee/top (poly) | 8 oz | USPS First-Class | $4–$6 |
| Dress/jeans (poly) | 12–15 oz | USPS First-Class | $5–$7 |
| Sweater/coat (poly) | 1–2 lb | USPS Ground Advantage | $7–$10 |
| Heavy/structured | 2–5 lb | USPS Priority Mail | $9–$14 |

Prep: steam, fold neatly, tissue paper to prevent wrinkles; air out / odor-neutralize before packing.

## Returns & policy (2025–2026)

- Options: no returns, or 14/30/60-day (buyer- or seller-paid).
- **Top Rated Plus** discount requires 30-day free returns.
- Even with "no returns," eBay's **Money Back Guarantee** still covers SNAD.
- **Authenticity Guarantee:** covers **select luxury brands** (and high-value categories like
  handbags, sneakers, watches), not vintage broadly. Don't promise Rob a no-name 70s dress
  qualifies — it's brand- and threshold-gated. Where it applies, experts authenticate and a
  failed check cancels the sale. (The two research reports disagreed on how wide this is; this
  is the conservative, accurate read — confirm a specific brand on eBay's AG page before relying.)

## Account health (defect-rate thresholds — both are real, they're different bars)
- **≤ 0.5%** transaction defect rate (and ≤ 3 defects from unique buyers) = **Top Rated Seller**.
- **≤ 2%** = stay "Above Standard"; cross 2% and you drop to **Below Standard** (search
  suppression + the +6–7% fee penalty). So 0.5% is the goal, 2% is the cliff — never confuse them.
- eBay evaluates on the 20th monthly: trailing 3 months if >400 sales in the last 3 months,
  otherwise trailing 12 months.
- Top Rated Sellers offering 30-day returns get stronger false-SNAD protection, shipping
  subsidy, and feedback removal.

## Common scams & countermeasures
| Scam | Countermeasure |
|---|---|
| Switcheroo (different item returned) | Photograph/record packaging; check return weight |
| SNAD fraud | Detailed listing + photos; respond fast with evidence |
| Empty-box return | Compare return weight; record an unboxing video |
| Wardrobing (worn then returned) | Document condition before shipping |
| Partial-refund manipulation | Insist on the formal return process |

You can appeal fraudulent returns with photo/video evidence (2024–2025 update).

**Policy notes:** Depop dropped its 10% commission (now 3.3% + $0.45). eBay Promoted Listings
increasingly necessary for visibility. Poshmark trialed lower fees, reverted to 20%.
Use cross-listing tools with **auto-delisting** to avoid double-selling.
