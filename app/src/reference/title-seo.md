# Titles & Cassini SEO

## 80-char title formula
```
[Brand/Designer] [Era/Decade] [Style/Type] [Key Features/Pattern/Material] [Color] [Size]
```
Examples:
- `Gunne Sax 70s Prairie Maxi Dress Lace Floral White Blue Boho Cottagecore 7`
- `Y2K Sheer Mesh Top Butterfly Print Pink Lettuce Edge Rave Club Kid Small`
- `Chanel 90s Boucle Wool Jacket Black Gold Buttons Boxy Blazer France 38`

Use all ~80 characters on searchable terms. "Vintage" is redundant when the decade is present.

## Item specifics — priority order (fill EVERY field)
1. Brand (or "Unbranded")
2. Size Type & Size — give modern sizing **and** point to measurements
3. Color
4. Material
5. Style
6. Department
7. Era / Decade
8. Pattern
9. Neckline
10. Sleeve Type
11. Country/Region of Manufacture
12. Condition

Missing item specifics **bury the listing** in Cassini — completeness is a ranking input, not
just metadata. Use the "Women's Vintage Clothing" category for true vintage.

## Cassini notes
- Cassini rewards complete item specifics, sell-through, and (indirectly) free shipping.
- The keyword in the title must be physically verifiable on the garment — accuracy protects
  against SNAD and keeps the listing surfaced for the right buyers.
